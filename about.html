About Hi, I'm Davidson S. A. Alaci

Dedicated Educator and Experienced Town Planner

I am a lecturer, registered town planner, and researcher with over 22 years of experience in Nigeria and Ethiopia. My work focuses on urban and regional planning, environmental planning, and climate change, with a strong commitment to mentoring students and young professionals.

Professional Journey

My career has spanned over two decades, during which I have held key academic positions, contributed to over 50 peer-reviewed publications, and led accreditation teams to various universities across Nigeria. I am particularly passionate about using my expertise to foster the next generation of urban planners and environmental stewards.

Core Competencies

Urban and Regional Planning

Extensive experience in teaching and researching urban studies, regional planning, and environmental impact assessment.

Climate Change and Resilience

Focus on developing strategies to mitigate the impacts of climate change in urban environments.

Mentorship

Dedicated to guiding students and young professionals through academic and career challenges, helping them achieve their goals.

Why Collaborate With Me

I bring a wealth of experience in both academia and practical urban planning. My approach combines rigorous research with hands-on mentoring, ensuring that my students and collaborators are well-prepared to tackle real-world challenges in the fields of urban and environmental planning.
